日本語漢語の優勢なアクセント型の分布 ー外来語と比較してー

李 墨彤
March 2017
プリプリント
日本語漢語と外来語両方の優勢なアクセント型に適用したランキング

要旨

Focusing on the distribution of accentual patterns in Sino-Japanese words, this paper mainly presents a standard Optimality-Theoretic analysis on the frequency asymmetry of Sino-Japanese words’ accents, and compares it with loanwords’ accentual patterns in Tokyo Japanese. Morphophonological constraints LexFt and MorFt separately utilized and quantity-sensitive constraint NonFin(L) newly introduced, this analysis proposes a constraint ranking that successfully generates dominant accentual patterns of both Sino-Japanese words and loanwords.
